Poutine

Savoury Potato Fries Topped with Cheese Curds and Gravy. I present ... the Poutine!

This Canadian staple will have you putting on your winter flannel and lace up your skates. It brings out the most inner Canadian in you to life with all it's glory!

Ingredients

  • Potato Fries (straight, crinkle, curly, waffle ... whatever you prefer!
  • Cheese Curds
  • Gravy

Directions

  1. Fill the air fryer with the fries until the whole base is covered.
  2. Cook fries until golden brown.
  3. While fries are cooking, make the gravy.
  4. Add the fries to a dish.
  5. Sprinkle the fries with the cheese curds.
  6. Pour the gravy on top of the cheese and fries.
  7. Enjoy, ya hoser!
