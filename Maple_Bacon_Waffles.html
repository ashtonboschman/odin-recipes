Maple Bacon Waffles

Scrumptious Waffles Topped with Canadian Bacon and Maple Syrup

Do you like warm and fluffy waffles? Now imagine how much better they would be with the sweet and salty flavours of Canadian bacon and maple syrup! That's right, these are THAT good!

Ingredients

  • Waffle Batter
  • Maple Syrup
  • Canadian Bacon

Directions

  1. Cook the Canadian bacon in a pan until nice and crispy.
  2. When bacon is done cooking, add 1 Cup of waffle batter to a waffle maker.
  3. Wait until the waffles are cooked (they should be fluffy and throughouly cooked).
  4. Repeat the process until desired amount of waffles are made.
  5. Stack waffles on top of eachother and add bacon to the very top.
  6. Smother the waffles and bacon in maple syrup.
  7. Enjoy, eh!
